# ENIGMA Consortium

## Overview
ENIGMA conducts coordinated neuroimaging and genetics studies across 45+ working groups and hundreds of sites using federated meta-analysis: sites share derived statistics rather than raw data, enabling population-scale power while preserving privacy. [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|Paris Brain Institute]] participates in multiple working groups.

## Key Outputs
- 200+ publications on brain structure in psychiatric and neurological disorders
- ENIGMA Toolbox (open-source surface-based analysis)
- Harmonised MRI acquisition protocols and processing pipelines
