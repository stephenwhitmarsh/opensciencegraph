# ReproNim - Reproducible Neuroimaging

## Overview
ReproNim is a NIH-funded centre dedicated to improving reproducibility in neuroimaging through tools, training, and standards. It builds on [[Standards/BIDS\|BIDS]] and [[Standards/NIDM\|NIDM]] to enable provenance tracking and computational re-executability across studies.

## Standards Developed
- [[Standards/NIDM\|NIDM]] — primary development home
- [[Standards/BIDS\|BIDS]] — builds and extends BIDS with provenance tooling (ReproIn, DataLad)

## Key Tools
- ReproIn - BIDS-compatible data acquisition
- DataLad - distributed data management
- ReproMan - computational environment management
- NIDM-Terms - shared neuroimaging vocabulary
