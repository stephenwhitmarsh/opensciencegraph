# INCF - International Neuroinformatics Coordinating Facility

## Overview
INCF coordinates neuroscience data standards and neuroinformatics infrastructure globally. It governs [[Standards/BIDS\|BIDS]], endorses [[Standards/NWB\|NWB]], [[Standards/HED\|HED]], and [[Standards/NIDM\|NIDM]], runs a network of national nodes (including a French node via [[Governance/CNRS Open Science\|CNRS Open Science]]), and provides the INCF TrainingSpace open neuroscience training catalog.

## Key Outputs
- Governance of [[Standards/BIDS\|BIDS]]
- Endorsement of [[Standards/NWB\|NWB]], [[Standards/HED\|HED]], [[Standards/NIDM\|NIDM]]
- INCF TrainingSpace - open neuroscience training
- INCF Standards and Best Practices collection

## Standards Governed
- [[Standards/BIDS\|BIDS]] — primary governing body
- [[Standards/NWB\|NWB]] — endorsing organisation
- [[Standards/HED\|HED]] — endorsing organisation
- [[Standards/NIDM\|NIDM]] — endorsing organisation via NIDASH working group
- [[Standards/NeuroML\|NeuroML]] — endorsing organisation via multiscale modeling program
