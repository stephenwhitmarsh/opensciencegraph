# Human Connectome Project (HCP)

## Overview
The HCP produced landmark open datasets of high-resolution structural and functional MRI, diffusion MRI, MEG, and EEG in 1,200 healthy adults. Freely available via ConnectomeDB, it influenced the design of [[Standards/BIDS\|BIDS]] and remains a reference dataset for neuroimaging methods development.

## Standards Influenced
- [[Standards/BIDS\|BIDS]] — original design shaped by HCP data architecture and acquisition protocols

## Key Outputs
- HCP Young Adult dataset (1,200 subjects, 3T and 7T MRI)
- HCP Aging and HCP Development datasets
- HCP MMP parcellation (multimodal brain atlas)
- Open-source preprocessing pipelines
