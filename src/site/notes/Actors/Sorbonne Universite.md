# Sorbonne Université

## Overview
Sorbonne Université is one of France's leading research-intensive universities, formed in 2018 through the merger of Paris-Sorbonne (Paris IV) and Pierre et Marie Curie University (UPMC / Paris VI). It is a founding and governing member of [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|Paris Brain Institute]], alongside [[Governance/CNRS Open Science\|CNRS Open Science]], [[Governance/Inserm Open Science\|Inserm Open Science]], and [[Governance/AP-HP\|AP-HP]]. Sorbonne Université hosts the Faculty of Medicine (Pitié-Salpêtrière) and the Faculty of Science and Engineering, making it central to the clinical and scientific ecosystem within which [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]] operates.

With 55,000+ students, 6,000+ researchers, and 100+ laboratories, Sorbonne Université is France's largest university by research output and a major European research institution. It holds a strong open science policy commitment formalised in its strategic plan.

## Open Science Policy
Sorbonne Université has formally committed to open science across four pillars:
- **Open access to publications** — mandatory deposit in HAL-Sorbonne for all publications; target 100% open access
- **Research data management** — institutional DMP support via [[Platforms/OPIDoR\|OPIDoR]]; recommended deposit in [[Platforms/Recherche Data Gouv\|Recherche Data Gouv]]
- **Open source software** — encouragement of Software Heritage deposits
- **Research integrity** — alignment with ALLEA European Code of Conduct

Full policy: https://www.sorbonne-universite.fr/recherche-et-innovation/lengagement-pour-la-science-ouverte

## Relationship to Paris Brain Institute
Sorbonne Université is one of [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]]'s five founding and supervising institutions:
- Provides academic affiliation for all [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]] research staff
- Co-governs [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]] alongside CNRS, Inserm, AP-HP, and the ICM Foundation
- Faculty of Medicine (Saint-Antoine and Pitié-Salpêtrière campuses) provides the hospital partnership base for clinical research at [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]]
- Masters and doctoral programmes in neuroscience and clinical research train [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]]'s next generation of researchers

## Key Research Structures
- **Institut du Cerveau (ICM)** — [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|Paris Brain Institute]] (brain and spine)
- **Institut de Myologie** — [[Actors/Institut de Myologie\|Institut de Myologie]] (neuromuscular diseases)
- **APHP.Sorbonne Université** — clinical research partnership with [[Governance/AP-HP\|AP-HP]]
- **Multiple UMR units** — joint CNRS/Inserm research units (UMR) hosted at Sorbonne Université campuses
