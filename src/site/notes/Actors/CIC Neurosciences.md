# CIC Neurosciences — Centre d'Investigation Clinique (INSERM CIC-1422)

## Overview
The Centre d'Investigation Clinique Neurosciences (CIC-1422) is a dedicated clinical research platform for neurological and psychiatric diseases, co-governed by **Inserm** and **[[Governance/AP-HP\|AP-HP]]** and physically located on the first floor of the [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]] building at Pitié-Salpêtrière Hospital. It provides the infrastructure, staff, and regulatory framework to conduct Phase I–III therapeutic trials and observational studies in close collaboration with the research teams of [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|Paris Brain Institute]] and the clinical neurology service of AP-HP's Pôle des Maladies du Système Nerveux.

CICs (Centres d'Investigation Clinique) are a national network created in 1992 by Inserm and the French Ministry of Health (DGOS) to bridge fundamental research and clinical application within university hospitals. The Pitié-Salpêtrière CIC Neurosciences is the neurology-specialist node of this network, one of ~30 CICs across France, and the French local partner of [[Governance/ECRIN\|ECRIN]] (European Clinical Research Infrastructure Network) via F-CRIN.

## Role and Activities
- **~80 clinical studies per year**, approximately half of which are therapeutic trials (Phase I/II/III)
- **Regulatory and ethics support** — preparation of CTA (Clinical Trial Authorisation) files for ANSM, CPP ethics submissions, CTIS coordination
- **Sponsor and CRO liaison** — interface between academic [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]] researchers (as investigators) and pharmaceutical/biotech sponsors
- **Patient recruitment** — access to the Pitié-Salpêtrière neurology ward and [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]] cohort populations (Alzheimer's, Parkinson's, ALS, epilepsy, rare neurological diseases)
- **Outcome measure development** — works with [[Governance/NeurATRIS\|NeurATRIS]] on Phase I/II trial endpoints for neurological biotherapies
- **Data management** — clinical trial data captured via [[Platforms/REDCap\|REDCap]] and [[Platforms/CleanWEB\|CleanWEB]]; data structured to [[Standards/CDISC\|CDISC]] standards for regulatory submissions

## Governance and Network
- **Dual supervision**: Inserm (scientific governance) and AP-HP (operational and financial)
- **Director**: Prof. Jean-Christophe Corvol (as of last confirmed update)
- **National network**: F-CRIN (French Clinical Research Infrastructure Network) — the French national partner of [[Governance/ECRIN\|ECRIN]]; the CIC-1422 is one of F-CRIN's neuroscience nodes
- **European network**: [[Governance/ECRIN\|ECRIN]] — supports multinational neurological disease trial coordination via F-CRIN
