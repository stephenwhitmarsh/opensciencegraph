# Actors

This index covers all organisational nodes in the graph: research institutes, international consortia, funding bodies, genomics organisations, open microscopy bodies, and ICM's own internal platforms and core facilities. The central node of the entire graph is [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|Paris Brain Institute]] (ICM), whose platforms and affiliations drive much of the network's topology. 

Filter using `type: institute / consortium / initiative / biobank` and `scope: french / european / international`. 

Return to [[MOC\|MOC]].

- [[Actors/ADNI\|ADNI]] — Alzheimer's Disease Neuroimaging Initiative; multisite open clinical neuroimaging cohort
- [[Actors/Allen Institute for Brain Science\|Allen Institute for Brain Science]] — co-founded NWB; open-access brain atlases and electrophysiology datasets
- [[Actors/Banque ADN et Cellules\|Banque ADN et Cellules]] — ICM DNA and cell biobank; BBMRI-ERIC; HPO/ORDO phenotyping; links to iGENSEQ and CATI
- [[Actors/BICAN\|BICAN]] — BRAIN Initiative Cell Atlas Network; comprehensive multi-resolution mammalian brain cell atlas
- [[Actors/Brain-MINDS\|Brain-MINDS]] — Japan's AMED-funded brain mapping programme; open marmoset atlas; SRPBS dataset on OpenNeuro
- [[Actors/CENIR\|CENIR]] — ICM neuroimaging centre; 3T MRI x2, PET-MRI, MEG; FLI node; data via CATI
- [[Actors/CONP\|CONP]] — Canadian Open Neuroscience Platform; federated BIDS portal; DataLad backend
- [[Actors/DAC\|DAC]] — ICM Data Analysis Core; bioinformatics, statistics, FAIR workflows; IFB/OPIDoR interface
- [[Actors/Donders Institute\|Donders Institute]] — European open-data pioneer; FieldTrip toolbox; mandatory data sharing policy
- [[Actors/DZNE\|DZNE]] — German Centre for Neurodegenerative Diseases; CURE-ND founding partner; EBRAINS contributor; JPND participant
- [[Actors/EMBL\|EMBL]] — European Molecular Biology Laboratory; operates EMBL-EBI (EGA, EVA, IDR, ENA, Ensembl)
- [[Actors/ENIGMA Consortium\|ENIGMA Consortium]] — federated meta-analysis across 45+ working groups; ICM participates
- [[Actors/GA4GH\|GA4GH]] — Global Alliance for Genomics and Health; governs VCF, Phenopackets, Beacon, DRS, DUO
- [[Actors/Human Connectome Project\|Human Connectome Project]] — landmark open MRI dataset; produced CIFTI format
- [[Actors/IBL\|IBL]] — International Brain Laboratory; fully open data/code model; Brain Wide Map on DANDI
- [[Actors/iGENSEQ\|iGENSEQ]] — ICM NGS platform; RNA-seq, scRNA-seq, WGS/WES; data to NCBI GEO / EGA / EVA
- [[Actors/INCF\|INCF]] — International Neuroinformatics Coordinating Facility; governs BIDS, endorses NWB/HED/NIDM
- [[Actors/Institut Curie\|Institut Curie]] — PICT-IBiSA imaging platform; France BioImaging node; QUAREP-LiMi participation
- [[Actors/Institut de Myologie\|Institut de Myologie]] — international reference centre for neuromuscular diseases; co-located at Pitie-Salpetriere; founding NeurATRIS node
- [[Actors/Institut Pasteur\|Institut Pasteur]] — founding France BioImaging node; Icy software; strong open science policy (90% OA)
- [[Actors/Mission Lucidity\|Mission Lucidity]] — imec/KU Leuven/VIB/UZ Leuven neurotechnology consortium (Belgium); CURE-ND founding partner
- [[Actors/Mount Sinai Neuroscience\|Mount Sinai Neuroscience]] — Friedman Brain Institute / Nash Family Dept; CommonMind + PsychENCODE lead site; ENIGMA
- [[Actors/NIH BRAIN Initiative\|NIH BRAIN Initiative]] — primary US funder of open neuroscience data infrastructure (DANDI, NWB mandate)
- [[Actors/OBI\|Actors/OBI]] — Ontario Brain Institute; FAIR neuroinformatics platform Brain-CODE; CONP founding sponsor
- [[Actors/OHDSI\|OHDSI]] — Observational Health Data Sciences and Informatics; maintains OMOP CDM; ATLAS, ACHILLES, HADES tools; 700M+ patient records globally
- [[Actors/OME\|OME]] — Open Microscopy Environment; produces OME-TIFF, OME-NGFF, Bio-Formats, OMERO
- [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|Paris Brain Institute]] — ICM; central node of this graph; hosts CENIR, CATI, iGENSEQ, DAC, Banque ADN et Cellules and 5 other platforms
- [[Actors/ReproNim\|ReproNim]] — reproducibility tools (NIDM, ReproIn, DataLad); NIH-funded
- [[Actors/Sorbonne Universite\|Sorbonne Universite]] — founding governing member of ICM; Faculty of Medicine at Pitie-Salpetriere; strong open science policy
- [[Actors/The Florey\|The Florey]] — largest brain research institute in southern hemisphere (Melbourne); ENIGMA-Epilepsy; ARDC ecosystem
- [[Actors/TOSI\|TOSI]] — Tanenbaum Open Science Institute (Montreal); first institute to adopt open science as core value
- [[Actors/UK Biobank\|UK Biobank]] — 500,000 participants; 50,000+ brain MRI; genomics; RAP cloud platform
- [[Actors/UK DRI\|UK DRI]] — UK Dementia Research Institute; CURE-ND founding partner; JPND; FAIR data policy
