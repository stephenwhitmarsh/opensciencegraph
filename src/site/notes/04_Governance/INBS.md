# INBS - Infrastructures Nationales en Biologie Santé

## Overview
INBS is the coordinating umbrella for French national research infrastructures
in biology and health, funded through the Investissements d'Avenir (PIA)
programme under CNRS coordination. It brings together 9 complementary national
platforms spanning genomics, bioinformatics, imaging, structural biology,
metabolomics, proteomics, phenotyping, and clinical research. INBS ensures
coordination between these infrastructures, supports their integration into
European networks (ESFRI, EOSC, ELIXIR), and provides a shared governance
framework aligned with [[04_Governance/Ouvrir la Science\|Ouvrir la Science]] and [[04_Governance/ANR Open Science Policy\|ANR Open Science Policy]].

## Member Infrastructures Relevant to Neuroscience
The full INBS network includes 9 national infrastructures. Those most directly
relevant to [[01_Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|Paris Brain Institute]] and open neuroscience are:

- **[[04_Governance/France BioImaging\|France BioImaging]]** (FBI) — biological and biomedical imaging at the
  cellular/subcellular scale; microscopy; French node of [[04_Governance/Euro-BioImaging\|Euro-BioImaging]]
- **[[04_Governance/France Life Imaging\|France Life Imaging]]** (FLI) — in vivo biomedical imaging (MRI, PET);
  [[04_Governance/CATI\|CATI]] is its neuroimaging data management node
- **[[04_Governance/IFB\|IFB]]** (Institut Français de Bioinformatique / ELIXIR-FR) — national
  bioinformatics infrastructure; French node of [[04_Governance/ELIXIR\|ELIXIR]]
- **FRISBI** (French Infrastructure for Integrated Structural Biology) —
  structural biology platforms
- **PHENOME** — plant phenotyping (less directly relevant)
- **METABOHUB** — metabolomics infrastructure
- **PROTEOMICS FRENCH INFRASTRUCTURE** (ProFI) — proteomics
- **BIOBANQUES** — French biobank network; connects to [[04_Governance/BBMRI-ERIC\|BBMRI-ERIC]]
- **IBISA** — network of technological platforms in biology and health

## Role in the French Open Science Ecosystem
INBS is the structural backbone linking French national biology/health
platforms to each other, to European ESFRI infrastructures, and to the
[[04_Governance/EOSC\|EOSC]]. It does not operate platforms directly but coordinates strategy,
access policies, data management frameworks, and European integration
across all member infrastructures.
