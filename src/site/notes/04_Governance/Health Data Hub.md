# Health Data Hub

## Overview
The Health Data Hub is the French national platform for health data, providing access
to the SNDS (Systeme National des Donnees de Sante, covering 99% of the French
population) via a GDPR-compliant secure Datalab. It uses [[02_Standards/OMOP CDM\|OMOP CDM]] and [[02_Standards/HL7 FHIR\|HL7 FHIR]]
for data standardisation and connects to [[04_Governance/AP-HP\|AP-HP]] hospital data.

## Key Resources
- SNDS - National Health Data System (insurance and hospital billing, 67M people)
- Datalab - secure cloud workspace for approved researchers
- Catalog of health datasets from multiple contributors

## Standards and Terminologies
- Data model: [[02_Standards/OMOP CDM\|OMOP CDM]], [[02_Standards/HL7 FHIR\|HL7 FHIR]]
- Clinical terminology: [[02_Standards/SNOMED CT\|SNOMED CT]]
- Diagnostic coding: [[02_Standards/ICD-10\|ICD-10]] / CIM-10 (SNDS source coding)
- Procedure coding: [[02_Standards/CCAM\|CCAM]] (SNDS source coding)
- Lab results: [[02_Standards/LOINC\|LOINC]]
- Rare diseases: [[02_Standards/ORDO\|ORDO]] (Orphanet codes in rare disease module)
- Data catalogue: [[02_Standards/DCAT\|DCAT]] / [[02_Standards/Dublin Core\|Dublin Core]] (dataset metadata)
