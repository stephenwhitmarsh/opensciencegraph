# RDA Neuroscience Interest Group

## Overview
The RDA Neuroscience Interest Group (IG) is a community of practice within the
**Research Data Alliance ([[04_Governance/RDA\|RDA]])** that bridges the broader RDA data management
community with neuroscience-specific data standards and infrastructure. It serves
as the meeting point between [[04_Governance/RDA\|RDA]]'s cross-domain working groups (on metadata,
provenance, data citation, FAIR) and the neuroscience standards bodies — primarily
[[01_Actors/INCF\|INCF]], [[02_Standards/BIDS\|BIDS]], [[02_Standards/NWB\|NWB]], and [[03_Platforms/EBRAINS\|EBRAINS]] — ensuring that neuroscience community
needs are represented in RDA outputs and that RDA standards are adopted in neuroscience
infrastructure.

## Role and Activities
- **Standards advocacy** — promotes adoption of [[02_Standards/BIDS\|BIDS]], [[02_Standards/NWB\|NWB]], and [[04_Governance/FAIR Principles\|FAIR Principles]]
  within the RDA community; ensures neuroscience use cases are represented in RDA
  working group outputs (e.g. FAIR Data Maturity Model, Persistent Identifiers WG)
- **Community bridge** — connects the open data management community ([[04_Governance/RDA\|RDA]],
  [[04_Governance/FORCE11\|FORCE11]], [[04_Governance/GO FAIR\|GO FAIR]]) with neuroscience data standards bodies ([[01_Actors/INCF\|INCF]],
  [[04_Governance/OHBM Open Science SIG\|OHBM Open Science SIG]], [[01_Actors/ENIGMA Consortium\|ENIGMA Consortium]])
- **FAIR neuroscience** — develops guidance on FAIR implementation specifically for
  neuroimaging and neurophysiology data, contributing to [[04_Governance/RDMkit\|RDMkit]] neuroscience content
- **Plenary outputs** — presents at RDA plenaries to disseminate neuroscience
  open data practices to the broader research data management community

## Key Connections to Standards
- [[02_Standards/BIDS\|BIDS]] — the RDA Neuroscience IG promotes BIDS as the FAIR-compliant organisation
  standard for neuroimaging data within the RDA ecosystem
- [[02_Standards/NWB\|NWB]] — promotes NWB for neurophysiology data FAIR sharing
- [[03_Platforms/EBRAINS\|EBRAINS]] — EBRAINS data sharing services are presented via the IG as an example
  of FAIR infrastructure implementation
- [[02_Standards/openMINDS\|openMINDS]] — EBRAINS metadata framework discussed within IG as neuroscience
  metadata best practice
