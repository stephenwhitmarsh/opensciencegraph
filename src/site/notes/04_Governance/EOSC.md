# EOSC - European Open Science Cloud

## Overview
EOSC is the EU's flagship open science infrastructure providing a federated ecosystem for research data, services, and tools across European research communities. It mandates [[04_Governance/FAIR Principles\|FAIR Principles]] for EU-funded research and integrates [[03_Platforms/EBRAINS\|EBRAINS]], [[04_Governance/OpenAIRE\|OpenAIRE]], [[04_Governance/ELIXIR\|ELIXIR]], and national infrastructures including French ones.
