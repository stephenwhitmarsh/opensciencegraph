# OHBM Open Science SIG

## Standards Promoted
- [[Standards/BIDS\|BIDS]] — community advocacy and adoption within OHBM
- [[Standards/NIDM\|NIDM]] — reproducibility standards advocacy

## Overview
The OHBM Open Science SIG promotes open science practices in the human brain mapping community, organising annual Brainhack events, producing open science educational resources, and advocating for [[Standards/BIDS\|BIDS]], [[Platforms/OpenNeuro\|OpenNeuro]], and reproducibility standards within OHBM.
