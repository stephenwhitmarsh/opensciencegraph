# Governance, Infrastructure and Frameworks

*[[MOC\|MOC]]*

This index covers all governance bodies, national and European research infrastructures, open science policies, working groups, and overarching frameworks in the graph. Sections move from French national scope outward to European and international levels, ending with thematic working groups that cut across scopes.

## French Open Science Policy and Mandates

- [[Governance/ANR Open Science Policy\|ANR Open Science Policy]] — mandates DMP, open access, [[Governance/FAIR Principles\|FAIR]] deposit for all ANR-funded projects
- [[Governance/Barometre Science Ouverte\|Barometre Science Ouverte]] — French Open Science Monitor; tracks OA rates and [[Governance/FAIR Principles\|FAIR]] data compliance
- [[Governance/CNRS Open Science\|CNRS Open Science]] — CNRS institutional open science policy; [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]] parent org
- [[Governance/CoSO\|CoSO]] — Comite pour la Science Ouverte; coordinates [[Governance/Ouvrir la Science\|Ouvrir la Science]] implementation; [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]] participates
- [[Governance/Inserm Open Science\|Inserm Open Science]] — Inserm institutional open science policy; [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]] parent org
- [[Governance/Ouvrir la Science\|Ouvrir la Science]] — French national open science plan (publications, data, software, skills)

## French Research Infrastructures

- [[Governance/France BioImaging\|France BioImaging]] — national cellular imaging infrastructure; French node of [[Governance/Euro-BioImaging\|Euro-BioImaging]]; [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]] participates
- [[Governance/France Life Imaging\|France Life Imaging]] — national in vivo imaging infrastructure; [[Actors/CATI\|CATI]] is its neuroimaging data node; [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]] participates
- [[Governance/IBiSA\|IBiSA]] — GIS for labelling and funding of technological platforms and CRBs in biology, health and agronomy; labels [[Actors/CENIR\|CENIR]] and [[Actors/iGENSEQ\|iGENSEQ]]
- [[Governance/IFB\|IFB]] — Institut Francais de Bioinformatique ([[Governance/ELIXIR\|ELIXIR]]-FR); FAIR-Checker; usegalaxy.fr; HPC
- [[Governance/INBS\|INBS]] — umbrella for all French national biology/health infrastructures ([[Governance/France BioImaging\|France BioImaging]], [[Governance/France Life Imaging\|France Life Imaging]], [[Governance/IFB\|IFB]], BIOBANQUES)
- [[Governance/MUDIS4LS\|MUDIS4LS]] — [[Governance/IFB\|IFB]]-coordinated FAIR digital infrastructure for French life sciences; multi-omics + imaging integration; [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]] participates
- [[Governance/NeurATRIS\|NeurATRIS]] — French translational neuroscience infrastructure; French node of [[Governance/EATRIS\|EATRIS]]; [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]] is core member; [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]] participates

## French Health Data and Legal Framework

- [[Governance/ANS\|ANS]] — Agence du Numerique en Sante; French health IT standardisation; [[Standards/SNOMED CT\|SNOMED CT]] NRC; [[Standards/HL7 FHIR\|HL7 FHIR]] French profiles
- [[Governance/AP-HP\|AP-HP]] — Assistance Publique Hopitaux de Paris; [[Platforms/EDS AP-HP\|EDS AP-HP]] ([[Standards/OMOP CDM\|OMOP CDM]]); [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]] partner via Pitie-Salpetriere
- [[Governance/CNIL\|CNIL]] — Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des Libertés; French independent data protection authority; issues authorisations and reference methodologies for health and genomic research data; GDPR supervisory authority; oversight of [[Platforms/Health Data Hub\|Health Data Hub]], [[Platforms/SNDS\|SNDS]], [[Actors/Banque ADN et Cellules\|Banque ADN et Cellules]] and all French health research data processing
- [[Governance/Code de la Sante Publique\|Code de la Sante Publique]] — French public health law; governs health data access, biobanking, clinical research

## Healthcare Interoperability

- [[Governance/IHE\|IHE]] — Integrating the Healthcare Enterprise; international initiative producing implementation profiles on top of [[Standards/HL7 FHIR\|HL7 FHIR]], [[Standards/DICOM\|DICOM]] and [[Standards/SNOMED CT\|SNOMED CT]]; IHE France coordinated via [[Governance/ANS\|ANS]]; foundational to [[Governance/EHDS\|EHDS]] technical compliance and [[Governance/AP-HP\|AP-HP]] clinical IT architecture

## Research Data, Publishing and Open Science Infrastructure

- [[Governance/cOAlition S\|cOAlition S]] — international funder consortium behind Plan S; mandates immediate open access for publicly funded research; ANR and ERC are members; Rights Retention Strategy; [[Platforms/Zenodo\|Zenodo]], [[Platforms/HAL\|HAL]] and [[Platforms/OpenAIRE\|OpenAIRE]] are compliant routes
- [[Governance/Huma-Num\|Huma-Num]] — French national digital humanities infrastructure; DARIAH and CLARIN French node

## European Research Infrastructures

- [[Governance/BBMRI-ERIC\|BBMRI-ERIC]] — European biobanking infrastructure; [[Standards/OMOP CDM\|OMOP CDM]], [[Standards/VCF\|VCF]], [[Standards/Phenopackets\|Phenopackets]]
- [[Governance/EATRIS\|EATRIS]] — European translational medicine infrastructure; biomarker development; clinical trial support
- [[Governance/ECRIN\|ECRIN]] — European Clinical Research Infrastructure Network; multinational trials; [[Standards/CDISC\|CDISC]]/[[Standards/OMOP CDM\|OMOP CDM]]/[[Standards/HL7 FHIR\|HL7 FHIR]]
- [[Governance/ELIXIR\|ELIXIR]] — European life science data infrastructure; 23 national nodes; French node = [[Governance/IFB\|IFB]]
- [[Governance/EOSC\|EOSC]] — European Open Science Cloud; mandates [[Governance/FAIR Principles\|FAIR]] for EU-funded research
- [[Governance/EOSC-Life\|EOSC-Life]] — Horizon 2020 consortium of 13 ESFRI life science infrastructures; produced WorkflowHub, LS Login and [[Platforms/RDMkit\|RDMkit]]; key participants include [[Governance/BBMRI-ERIC\|BBMRI-ERIC]], [[Governance/EATRIS\|EATRIS]], [[Governance/ECRIN\|ECRIN]], [[Governance/ELIXIR\|ELIXIR]], [[Actors/EMBL\|EMBL]], [[Governance/Euro-BioImaging\|Euro-BioImaging]]
- [[Governance/Euro-BioImaging\|Euro-BioImaging]] — European bioimaging infrastructure; French node = [[Governance/France BioImaging\|France BioImaging]]

## European Policy and Regulation

- [[Governance/EC Open Science Policy\|EC Open Science Policy]] — Horizon Europe OA mandates, Plan S, [[Governance/EOSC\|EOSC]] policy; drives all EU open science requirements
- [[Governance/EHDS\|EHDS]] — European Health Data Space Regulation (EU 2025/327); [[Standards/HL7 FHIR\|HL7 FHIR]] mandated; in force March 2025

## European Neuroscience Alliances

- [[Governance/CURE-ND\|CURE-ND]] — [[Actors/DZNE\|DZNE]] / [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]] / [[Actors/Mission Lucidity\|Mission Lucidity]] / [[Actors/UK DRI\|UK DRI]] alliance for neurodegeneration; [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]] founding member; [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]] participates
- [[Governance/JPND\|JPND]] — Joint Programme Neurodegenerative Disease Research; transnational funding; links [[Actors/DZNE\|DZNE]], [[Actors/UK DRI\|UK DRI]], [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]]
- [[Governance/LifeTime Initiative\|LifeTime Initiative]] — single-cell technologies for disease interception; multi-omics + imaging

## International Frameworks and Principles

- [[Governance/ARDC\|ARDC]] — Australian Research Data Commons; national FAIR/RDM infrastructure; [[Governance/EOSC\|EOSC]] counterpart; [[Governance/RDA\|RDA]] partner
- [[Governance/FAIR Principles\|FAIR Principles]] — 15 Findable/Accessible/Interoperable/Reusable principles (Wilkinson et al. 2016)
- [[Governance/GFRN\|GFRN]] — Global Federation of Reproducibility Networks; includes French RFRR network
- [[Governance/HDR UK\|HDR UK]] — Health Data Research UK; UK national health data science institute; [[Governance/FAIR Principles\|FAIR]]; [[Standards/OMOP CDM\|OMOP CDM]]; [[Standards/HL7 FHIR\|HL7 FHIR]]; [[Governance/CURE-ND\|CURE-ND]] AI/ML workshop participant
- [[Governance/RDA\|RDA]] — Research Data Alliance; FAIR Data Maturity Model; 120+ working groups
- [[Governance/UNESCO Open Science Recommendation\|UNESCO Open Science Recommendation]] — 2021 global framework for open science policy

## Clinical Research Infrastructure

- [[Governance/TransCelerate\|TransCelerate]] — pharma industry consortium; [[Standards/CDISC\|CDISC]] standards; trial data sharing
- [[Governance/YODA Project\|YODA Project]] — Yale Open Data Access; independent third-party clinical trial data sharing (499 trials); influenced [[Actors/NIH BRAIN Initiative\|NIH]] data sharing policy; co-founders created medRxiv

## Working Groups

- [[Governance/BIDS Steering Group\|BIDS Steering Group]] — governs [[Standards/BIDS\|BIDS]] specification and BEP extension process (under [[Actors/INCF\|INCF]])
- [[Governance/GT-GeDeM\|GT-GeDeM]] — [[Governance/RTmfm\|RTmfm]]/CNRS working group on microscopy data management; [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|ICM]] participates
- [[Governance/INCF Working Groups\|INCF Working Groups]] — [[Actors/INCF\|INCF]] task forces producing [[Standards/BIDS\|BIDS]], [[Standards/NWB\|NWB]], [[Standards/HED\|HED]], [[Standards/NIDM\|NIDM]], [[Standards/NeuroML\|NeuroML]]
- [[Governance/NWB Working Group\|NWB Working Group]] — governs [[Standards/NWB\|NWB]] specification and NDX extensions (under [[Actors/INCF\|INCF]])
- [[Governance/OBO Foundry\|OBO Foundry]] — Open Biological and Biomedical Ontology Foundry; governing consortium for interoperable biomedical ontologies ([[Standards/GO\|GO]], [[Standards/HPO\|HPO]], [[Standards/UBERON\|UBERON]], [[Standards/MONDO\|MONDO]], [[Standards/ORDO\|ORDO]], [[Standards/OBI\|OBI]], [[Standards/Cell Ontology\|Cell Ontology]])
- [[Governance/OHBM Open Science SIG\|OHBM Open Science SIG]] — promotes open science within OHBM; organises Brainhack
- [[Governance/QUAREP-LiMi\|QUAREP-LiMi]] — Quality Assessment and Reproducibility for light microscopy; 590+ members; ISO standards
- [[Governance/RDA Neuroscience IG\|RDA Neuroscience IG]] — bridges [[Governance/RDA\|RDA]] data management community with neuroscience standards
- [[Governance/RTmfm\|RTmfm]] — CNRS thematic network for functional multi-scale microscopy; parent body of [[Governance/GT-GeDeM\|GT-GeDeM]]; coordinates with [[Governance/France BioImaging\|France BioImaging]] and [[Governance/QUAREP-LiMi\|QUAREP-LiMi]]
