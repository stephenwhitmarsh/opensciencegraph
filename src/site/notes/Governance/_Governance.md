# Governance, Infrastructure and Frameworks

*[[MOC\|MOC]]*

This index covers all governance bodies, national and European research infrastructures, open science policies, working groups, and overarching frameworks in the graph. Sections move from French national scope outward to European and international levels, ending with thematic working groups that cut across scopes.

---

## French Open Science Policy and Mandates

- [[Governance/ANR Open Science Policy\|ANR Open Science Policy]] — mandates DMP, open access, FAIR deposit for all ANR-funded projects
- [[Governance/Barometre Science Ouverte\|Barometre Science Ouverte]] — French Open Science Monitor; tracks OA rates and FAIR data compliance
- [[Governance/CNRS Open Science\|CNRS Open Science]] — CNRS institutional open science policy; ICM parent org
- [[Governance/CoSO\|CoSO]] — Comite pour la Science Ouverte; coordinates Ouvrir la Science implementation; ICM participates
- [[Governance/Inserm Open Science\|Inserm Open Science]] — Inserm institutional open science policy; ICM parent org
- [[Governance/Ouvrir la Science\|Ouvrir la Science]] — French national open science plan (publications, data, software, skills)

## French Research Infrastructures

- [[Governance/CATI\|CATI]] — Centre d'Acquisition et de Traitement des Images; hosted at ICM; FLI neuroimaging node
- [[Governance/France BioImaging\|France BioImaging]] — national cellular imaging infrastructure; French node of Euro-BioImaging; ICM participates
- [[Governance/France Life Imaging\|France Life Imaging]] — national in vivo imaging infrastructure; CATI is its neuroimaging data node; ICM participates
- [[Governance/IFB\|IFB]] — Institut Francais de Bioinformatique (ELIXIR-FR); FAIR-Checker; usegalaxy.fr; HPC
- [[Governance/INBS\|INBS]] — umbrella for all French national biology/health infrastructures (FBI, FLI, IFB, BIOBANQUES)
- [[Governance/MUDIS4LS\|MUDIS4LS]] — IFB-coordinated FAIR digital infrastructure for French life sciences; multi-omics + imaging integration; ICM participates
- [[Governance/NeurATRIS\|NeurATRIS]] — French translational neuroscience infrastructure; French node of EATRIS; ICM is core member; ICM participates

## French Health Data and Legal Framework

- [[Governance/ANS\|ANS]] — Agence du Numerique en Sante; French health IT standardisation; SNOMED CT NRC; HL7 FHIR French profiles
- [[Governance/AP-HP\|AP-HP]] — Assistance Publique Hopitaux de Paris; EDS AP-HP (OMOP CDM); ICM partner via Pitie-Salpetriere
- [[Governance/Code de la Sante Publique\|Code de la Sante Publique]] — French public health law; governs health data access, biobanking, clinical research
- [[Governance/Health Data Hub\|Health Data Hub]] — French national health data platform; SNDS; OMOP CDM; EHDS national HDAB candidate

## French Research Data and Publication Infrastructure

- [[Governance/data.gouv.fr\|data.gouv.fr]] — general French government open data portal; umbrella for Recherche Data Gouv and HDH
- [[Governance/HAL\|HAL]] — French national open access publication archive; mandatory deposit under ANR/CNRS/Inserm mandates
- [[Governance/Huma-Num\|Huma-Num]] — French national digital humanities infrastructure; DARIAH and CLARIN French node
- [[Governance/OPIDoR\|OPIDoR]] — DMP OPIDoR, Cat OPIDoR, DORANum, JurisDoR; national RDM tools suite
- [[Governance/Software Heritage\|Software Heritage]] — universal software source code archive; French open science mandate for code deposit

## European Research Infrastructures

- [[Governance/BBMRI-ERIC\|BBMRI-ERIC]] — European biobanking infrastructure; OMOP CDM, VCF, Phenopackets
- [[Governance/EATRIS\|EATRIS]] — European translational medicine infrastructure; biomarker development; clinical trial support
- [[Governance/ECRIN\|ECRIN]] — European Clinical Research Infrastructure Network; multinational trials; CDISC/OMOP/FHIR
- [[Governance/ELIXIR\|ELIXIR]] — European life science data infrastructure; 23 national nodes; French node = IFB
- [[Governance/EOSC\|EOSC]] — European Open Science Cloud; mandates FAIR for EU-funded research
- [[Governance/Euro-BioImaging\|Euro-BioImaging]] — European bioimaging infrastructure; French node = France BioImaging
- [[Governance/Human Brain Project\|Human Brain Project]] — EU Horizon Flagship (2013-2023); created EBRAINS
- [[Governance/OpenAIRE\|OpenAIRE]] — EU open science and open access infrastructure; hosts Zenodo

## European Policy and Regulation

- [[Governance/EC Open Science Policy\|EC Open Science Policy]] — Horizon Europe OA mandates, Plan S, EOSC policy; drives all EU open science requirements
- [[Governance/EHDS\|EHDS]] — European Health Data Space Regulation (EU 2025/327); HL7 FHIR mandated; in force March 2025

## European Neuroscience Alliances

- [[Governance/CURE-ND\|CURE-ND]] — DZNE / ICM / Mission Lucidity / UK DRI alliance for neurodegeneration; ICM founding member; ICM participates
- [[Governance/JPND\|JPND]] — Joint Programme Neurodegenerative Disease Research; transnational funding; links DZNE, UK DRI, ICM
- [[Governance/LifeTime Initiative\|LifeTime Initiative]] — single-cell technologies for disease interception; multi-omics + imaging

## International Frameworks and Principles

- [[Governance/ARDC\|ARDC]] — Australian Research Data Commons; national FAIR/RDM infrastructure; EOSC counterpart; RDA partner
- [[Governance/FAIR Principles\|FAIR Principles]] — 15 Findable/Accessible/Interoperable/Reusable principles (Wilkinson et al. 2016)
- [[Governance/FORCE11\|FORCE11]] — co-authored FAIR Principles; scholarly communications reform
- [[Governance/GFRN\|GFRN]] — Global Federation of Reproducibility Networks; includes French RFRR network
- [[Governance/GO FAIR\|GO FAIR]] — international initiative for FAIR implementation; Three-Point FAIRification Framework
- [[Governance/HDR UK\|HDR UK]] — Health Data Research UK; UK national health data science institute; FAIR; OMOP CDM; HL7 FHIR; CURE-ND AI/ML workshop participant
- [[Governance/RDA\|RDA]] — Research Data Alliance; FAIR Data Maturity Model; 120+ working groups
- [[Governance/UNESCO Open Science Recommendation\|UNESCO Open Science Recommendation]] — 2021 global framework for open science policy

## Clinical Research Infrastructure

- [[Governance/i2b2\|i2b2]] — clinical data warehousing platform; deployed at AP-HP alongside OMOP CDM
- [[Governance/TransCelerate\|TransCelerate]] — pharma industry consortium; CDISC standards; trial data sharing
- [[Governance/YODA Project\|YODA Project]] — Yale Open Data Access; independent third-party clinical trial data sharing (499 trials); influenced NIH data sharing policy; co-founders created medRxiv

## Working Groups

- [[Governance/BIDS Steering Group\|BIDS Steering Group]] — governs BIDS specification and BEP extension process (under INCF)
- [[Governance/GT-GeDeM\|GT-GeDeM]] — RTmfm/CNRS working group on microscopy data management; ICM participates
- [[Governance/INCF Working Groups\|INCF Working Groups]] — INCF task forces producing BIDS, NWB, HED, NIDM, NeuroML
- [[Governance/NIF\|NIF]] — Neuroscience Information Framework; RRID identifiers for research resources; repository registry
- [[Governance/NWB Working Group\|NWB Working Group]] — governs NWB specification and NDX extensions (under INCF)
- [[Governance/OHBM Open Science SIG\|OHBM Open Science SIG]] — promotes open science within OHBM; organises Brainhack
- [[Governance/QUAREP-LiMi\|QUAREP-LiMi]] — Quality Assessment and Reproducibility for light microscopy; 590+ members; ISO standards
- [[Governance/RDA Neuroscience IG\|RDA Neuroscience IG]] — bridges RDA data management community with neuroscience standards
- [[Governance/RDMkit\|RDMkit]] — ELIXIR research data management toolkit; life sciences FAIR guidance; French resources via IFB
