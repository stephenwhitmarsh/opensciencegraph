# Governance, Infrastructure and Frameworks

This index covers all governance bodies, national and European research infrastructures, open science policies, working groups, and overarching frameworks in the graph. Nodes are scoped as `french`, `european`, or `international`. French nodes include national open science policies (ANR, CNRS, Inserm), research infrastructures (IFB, France BioImaging, France Life Imaging, INBS, NeurATRIS), and health data governance (Health Data Hub, AP-HP, ANS). European nodes include the major transnational infrastructures (EOSC, EBRAINS, ELIXIR, Euro-BioImaging, EATRIS, ECRIN) and the EHDS regulation. International nodes cover the FAIR Principles, RDA, UNESCO, and key global reproducibility and data management frameworks. 

Filter using `scope: french / european / international` and `type: policy / infrastructure / working-group / framework`.
