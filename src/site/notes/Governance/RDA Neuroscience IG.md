# RDA Neuroscience Interest Group

## Overview
The RDA Neuroscience Interest Group (IG) is a community of practice within the **Research Data Alliance ([[Governance/RDA\|RDA]])** that bridges the broader RDA data management community with neuroscience-specific data standards and infrastructure. It serves as the meeting point between [[Governance/RDA\|RDA]]'s cross-domain working groups (on metadata,
provenance, data citation, FAIR) and the neuroscience standards bodies — primarily [[Actors/INCF\|INCF]], [[Standards/BIDS\|BIDS]], [[Standards/NWB\|NWB]], and [[Platforms/EBRAINS\|EBRAINS]] — ensuring that neuroscience community needs are represented in RDA outputs and that RDA standards are adopted in neuroscience infrastructure.

## Role and Activities
- **Standards advocacy** — promotes adoption of [[Standards/BIDS\|BIDS]], [[Standards/NWB\|NWB]], and [[Governance/FAIR Principles\|FAIR Principles]] within the RDA community; ensures neuroscience use cases are represented in RDA working group outputs (e.g. FAIR Data Maturity Model, Persistent Identifiers WG)
- **Community bridge** — connects the open data management community ([[Governance/RDA\|RDA]], [[Governance/FORCE11\|FORCE11]], [[Governance/GO FAIR\|GO FAIR]]) with neuroscience data standards bodies ([[Actors/INCF\|INCF]], [[Governance/OHBM Open Science SIG\|OHBM Open Science SIG]], [[Actors/ENIGMA Consortium\|ENIGMA Consortium]])
- **FAIR neuroscience** — develops guidance on FAIR implementation specifically for neuroimaging and neurophysiology data, contributing to [[Governance/RDMkit\|RDMkit]] neuroscience content
- **Plenary outputs** — presents at RDA plenaries to disseminate neuroscience open data practices to the broader research data management community

## Key Connections to Standards
- [[Standards/BIDS\|BIDS]] — the RDA Neuroscience IG promotes BIDS as the FAIR-compliant organisation standard for neuroimaging data within the RDA ecosystem
- [[Standards/NWB\|NWB]] — promotes NWB for neurophysiology data FAIR sharing
- [[Platforms/EBRAINS\|EBRAINS]] — EBRAINS data sharing services are presented via the IG as an example of FAIR infrastructure implementation
- [[Standards/openMINDS\|openMINDS]] — EBRAINS metadata framework discussed within IG as neuroscience metadata best practice
