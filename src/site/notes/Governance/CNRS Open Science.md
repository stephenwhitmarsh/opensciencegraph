# CNRS Open Science

## Standards Infrastructure
- [[Actors/INCF\|INCF]] — operates French national node
- [[Governance/RDA\|RDA]] — operates French national node

## National Infrastructure
- [[Governance/INBS\|INBS]] — coordinates French national biology/health platforms
- [[Governance/IFB\|IFB]] — French bioinformatics infrastructure (ELIXIR-FR)
- [[Governance/France BioImaging\|France BioImaging]] — French microscopy infrastructure

## Overview
CNRS is the primary research partner of [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|Paris Brain Institute]] and operates the French national nodes of [[Actors/INCF\|INCF]] and [[Governance/RDA\|RDA]]. Its open science policy mandates DMPs, open access via HAL-CNRS, and FAIR data via [[Platforms/Recherche Data Gouv\|Recherche Data Gouv]].
