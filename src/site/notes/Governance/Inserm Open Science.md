# Inserm Open Science

## Overview
Inserm is the French national biomedical research institute and a founding partner of [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|Paris Brain Institute]]. Its open science policy aligns with [[Governance/Ouvrir la Science\|Ouvrir la Science]] and is operationalised by the **Département de la Science Ouverte (DSO)**, created in July 2023 (https://pro.inserm.fr/rubriques/en-labo/science-ouverte/la-science-ouverte-a-linserm). The DSO drives Inserm's institutional open science policy in line with national ([[Governance/CoSO\|CoSO]]) and European ([[Governance/cOAlition S\|cOAlition S]]) orientations, provides tools and services to researchers, and produces bibliometric indicators for scientific evaluation. The policy is formalised in the **Charte de la science ouverte de l'Inserm** (September 2024, https://pro.inserm.fr/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/CharteScienceOuverteInserm-WCAG.pdf).

## Key Policy Commitments
- **Open access to publications** — mandatory [[Platforms/HAL\|HAL]] deposit of all publications from 2020; HAL-Inserm institutional portal (https://hal.inserm.fr) active since 2006; ~81% of Inserm publications are open access (internal barometer)
- **FAIR research data** — institutional partition on [[Platforms/Recherche Data Gouv\|Recherche Data Gouv]] opened March 2023; DMP templates available via [[Platforms/OPIDoR\|OPIDoR]]; "as open as possible, as closed as necessary" principle
- **Open source software** — encourages deposit in [[Platforms/Software Heritage\|Software Heritage]] and [[Platforms/Zenodo\|Zenodo]]
- **Research evaluation reform** — DORA signatory since 2018; co-chairs the French chapter of CoARA (Coalition for Advancing Research Assessment) since 2024; HAL deposits used as basis for researcher evaluation

## DSO Tools and Services
- **HAL-Inserm** (https://hal.inserm.fr) — Inserm's institutional portal on the national [[Platforms/HAL\|HAL]] archive; mandatory deposit destination
- **InsermBiblio** (https://insermbiblio.inserm.fr) — Inserm's digital library providing full-text access to journals and bibliographic tools
- **MeSH bilingue** (https://mesh.inserm.fr) — Inserm's French translation of the NLM [[Standards/MeSH\|MeSH]] thesaurus; updated annually
- **Service Open Science (SOS)** (https://sos.inserm.fr) — platform to inform and support researchers in open science practices
- **iPubli** (https://www.ipubli.inserm.fr) — Inserm editorial production platform for open access publications
- **DMP templates on [[Platforms/OPIDoR\|OPIDoR]]** (https://dmp.opidor.fr) — Inserm-specific DMP templates aligned with [[Governance/ANR Open Science Policy\|ANR Open Science Policy]] requirements
