# Inserm Open Science

## Overview
Inserm is the French national biomedical research institute and a founding partner of [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|Paris Brain Institute]]. Its open science policy aligns with [[Governance/Ouvrir la Science\|Ouvrir la Science]] and includes governance of [[Governance/Health Data Hub\|Health Data Hub]] and the national CIC clinical investigation centre network (including at [[Governance/AP-HP\|AP-HP]]).

## Connections
- Implements: [[Governance/Ouvrir la Science\|Ouvrir la Science]]
- Part of: [[Governance/CoSO\|CoSO]], [[Governance/Health Data Hub\|Health Data Hub]] (governance board)
- Partner: [[Actors/Paris Brain Institute\|Paris Brain Institute]], [[Governance/AP-HP\|AP-HP]], [[Governance/CATI\|CATI]]
