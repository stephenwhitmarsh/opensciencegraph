# Zenodo

## Overview
Zenodo is a general-purpose open research repository (CERN / [[04_Governance/OpenAIRE\|OpenAIRE]]). It accepts any research output up to 50 GB per deposit, assigns DOIs, and provides long-term preservation. Recommended for EU-funded projects and by [[04_Governance/ANR Open Science Policy\|ANR Open Science Policy]].
