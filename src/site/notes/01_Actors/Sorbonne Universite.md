# Sorbonne Université

## Overview
Sorbonne Université is one of France's leading research-intensive universities,
formed in 2018 through the merger of Paris-Sorbonne (Paris IV) and Pierre et
Marie Curie University (UPMC / Paris VI). It is a founding and governing member
of [[00_Core/Paris Brain Institute\|Paris Brain Institute]], alongside [[04_Governance/CNRS Open Science\|CNRS Open Science]], [[04_Governance/Inserm Open Science\|Inserm Open Science]],
and [[04_Governance/AP-HP\|AP-HP]]. Sorbonne Université hosts the Faculty of Medicine (Pitié-Salpêtrière)
and the Faculty of Science and Engineering, making it central to the clinical
and scientific ecosystem within which ICM operates.

With 55,000+ students, 6,000+ researchers, and 100+ laboratories, Sorbonne Université
is France's largest university by research output and a major European research
institution. It holds a strong open science policy commitment formalised in its
strategic plan.

## Open Science Policy
Sorbonne Université has formally committed to open science across four pillars:
- **Open access to publications** — mandatory deposit in HAL-Sorbonne for all
  publications; target 100% open access
- **Research data management** — institutional DMP support via [[04_Governance/OPIDoR\|OPIDoR]];
  recommended deposit in [[03_Platforms/Recherche Data Gouv\|Recherche Data Gouv]]
- **Open source software** — encouragement of Software Heritage deposits
- **Research integrity** — alignment with ALLEA European Code of Conduct

Full policy: https://www.sorbonne-universite.fr/recherche-et-innovation/lengagement-pour-la-science-ouverte

## Relationship to Paris Brain Institute
Sorbonne Université is one of ICM's five founding and supervising institutions:
- Provides academic affiliation for all ICM research staff
- Co-governs ICM alongside CNRS, Inserm, AP-HP, and the ICM Foundation
- Faculty of Medicine (Saint-Antoine and Pitié-Salpêtrière campuses) provides
  the hospital partnership base for clinical research at ICM
- Masters and doctoral programmes in neuroscience and clinical research train
  ICM's next generation of researchers

## Key Research Structures
- **Institut du Cerveau (ICM)** — [[00_Core/Paris Brain Institute\|Paris Brain Institute]] (brain and spine)
- **Institut de Myologie** — [[01_Actors/Institut de Myologie\|Institut de Myologie]] (neuromuscular diseases)
- **APHP.Sorbonne Université** — clinical research partnership with [[04_Governance/AP-HP\|AP-HP]]
- **Multiple UMR units** — joint CNRS/Inserm research units (UMR) hosted at
  Sorbonne Université campuses
