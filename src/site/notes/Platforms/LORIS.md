# LORIS - Longitudinal Online Research and Imaging System

## Overview
LORIS is an open-source web-based data management system for neuroimaging and clinical research, supporting multisite longitudinal studies from DICOM ingestion through QC, [[Standards/BIDS\|BIDS]] conversion, and public release. Used in [[Actors/ADNI\|ADNI]] and [[Actors/CONP\|CONP]].
